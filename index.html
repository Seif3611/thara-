About Us

"THARA is a one-of-a-kind cultural and entertainment destination in the heart of Taba, South Sinai."

More than just a place to stay, THARA where ancient stories, local art, and natural beauty come together. Whether you're seeking relaxation, exploration, or cultural discovery, our resort offers a wide range of activities, performances, and interactive spaces designed to spark curiosity and connection. At THARA , we believe in creating meaningful moments — from serene mornings overlooking the sea to lively evenings filled with music, flavors, and folklore. Every element of THARA is crafted to reflect the soul of Sinai while delivering the highest standards of hospitality and comfort.
